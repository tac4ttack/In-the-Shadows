<size=150%><u>In The Shadows</u></size>


A <color=#4490A9><i>42 school</i></color> project for the <color=#4490A9><i>Unity3D Engine</i></color> course.

The goal was to recreate a small game prototype like <color=#4490A9><i>Shadowmatic</i></color>, a puzzle game made for mobile devices in 2015.

More info about the original game at <color=#4490A9><i><size=60%>http://www.shadowmatic.com</size></i></color>.

Project's page: <color=#4490A9><i><size=60%>https://tacattack.itch.io/in-the-shadows</size></i></color>

Source code: <color=#4490A9><i><size=60%>https://github.com/tac4ttack/In-the-Shadows/</size></i></color>
